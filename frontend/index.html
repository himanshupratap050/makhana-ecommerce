Founder's Note

Why I Started
MakhanaBliss

Growing up in Bihar, makhana was always part of our lives — eaten during fasts, made into kheer, roasted on winter evenings. But when I moved to a city, I couldn't find the same pure, fresh makhana anywhere.

Everything in stores was either stale, overpriced, or loaded with additives. That's when I decided to go back to my roots — literally. I partnered directly with farmers in Mithila and started MakhanaBliss.

Today, every pack you order comes straight from those same trusted farmers, processed with zero chemicals, and delivered fresh to your door.

— Founder, MakhanaBliss
